who am i

The beginning

My name is Ngoc Duc van Valen and I’m 21 years old. I live in Ha Noi, the Viet Nam and I studied Graphic design here. Before my education, I’ve always been creating, but not just photo-editing. I made digital music, video’s, photo’s, I even did dancing, I played the Football, Volleyball all all other sports.


You see, I’ve always been busy doing creative stuff in my free time. However, I didn’t know Photoshop. Or… well, I knew it, I just didn’t like it all that much. However, two years ago I started my study and got pushed to work with Adobe Photoshop. After one hour of doing random stuff, I absolutely fell in love with it. Sadly, they didn’t teach me a lot about Photoshop. That’s why I started learning myself, just by using it a LOT! And my life changed

What I do

I'm a Graphic Designer and Digital Artist, Web Design. On my computer I do things like Editing your photos, 'Realistifying' your Drawings, unique challenges, editing races, and so much more!

My destination

I want to discover many new things that I can't do on my own, I want to be a conqueror for my life, discover new people, want to go to a new land and do amazing things. great for this world
